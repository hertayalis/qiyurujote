Bradley Cooper and Irina Shayk Hit the Beach with Their Baby

Publish date: 2024-08-18

The Cooper-Shayk family has been killing it with their public photo ops lately, from pumpkin patch visits to causal outings in LA, but their most recent appearance takes the cake.

The trio—Bradley, Irina, and baby Lea de Seine—was spotted enjoying a beach day at Sunset Beach, LA on Tuesday, and they were too cute to handle.

The proud first-time parents were all smiles while watching their little one play in the sand. While her mom and dad dressed down in dark colors, Lea de Seine wore a white long sleeve shirt and pants.

MEGA

Cooper and Shayk lounged in the sun, snuggled, and carried around their baby girl, according to other photos on TMZ. But couple couldn't avoid the dirty work—they also had to change some diapers during the trip.

Looking picture-perfect on the beach isn't new for the Cooper-Shayk clan. The family of three was previously seen happily taking a stroll on the shore back in October. We need these photos printed onto postcards ASAP.

The infant was most recently seen out with her mother last week, elsewhere in Los Angeles. She's not even one year old yet, but she's already had mommy-and-me dates across the country, thanks to her jetsetting model mom. Last fall, the two were seen out in New York together between shows during NYFW. We wonder if the stylish tot will head to the east coast again for this fashion week.

Lea de Seine will celebrate her first birthday at the end of March.
