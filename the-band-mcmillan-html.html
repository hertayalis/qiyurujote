The Band McMillan - Members, Ages, Trivia

Publish date: 2024-06-23

Trio of siblings who perform country music as a family band. They have released songs such as "Somebody's Got To" and "Letter to the World." They are popular on social media with more than 1.1 million fans on TikTok alone. 

Trivia

Through their TikTok account they have hosted giveaways with prizes that include Air Pods and hoodies. 

Members

The band features Lauren, Kathryn, and Robert McMillan.
