Where is the Learn button on a Genie Blue Max garage door opener?

Publish date: 2024-07-02

Answer

Depending on the model of garage door opener you own, “Learn Code” is typically located near the antenna of the unit. Press and release “Learn Code”; the LED indicator will blink at a rate of twice per second.

Also, how do you programme a genie Blue Max garage door opener?

Press and hold down the square button on the motor head until the round blue LED comes on, then release the button.

To unlock the programming feature use a remote that already operates the unit and press it once.

Hold the new remote 4 or 5 feet away and press 3, 4 or 5 times until the door activates.

Beside above, how do I reset my genie Intellicode?

This keypad is black with a slide-up cover and the word “Intellicode” printed on the cover. To reset this keypad, open cover half way, then press and hold the programme and “8” buttons simultaneously while opening the cover completely. The indicator light will blink once and then go out.

Besides, are Genie garage door openers universal?

The Genie universal garage door opener remote is unique because it is designed to “Teach & Pair” your existing garage door opener remote. This programming process is patented by Genie and is designed to make this one of the easiest garage door opener remote setups available.
