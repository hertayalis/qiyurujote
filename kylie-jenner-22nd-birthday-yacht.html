Kylie Jenner Rents Luxurious $250 Million Yacht for Her Birthday

Publish date: 2024-06-22
  • For her upcoming 22nd birthday, Kylie Jenner reportedly rented a $250 million yacht that’s almost the length of a football field.
  • The yacht, named Tranquility, costs approximately $1,249,500 per week.
  • Amenities include a helipad, theater, sauna, beauty salon, and Turkish bath.

To casually flex and make all of us feel worse about our Monday, Kylie Jenner has reportedly rented a yacht the length of a football field to celebrate her 22nd birthday. According to TMZ, the $250 million yacht, aptly named the Tranquility, is longer than 300 feet and will be home to Jenner’s next big bash.

The floating luxury house accommodates 22 guests and features deluxe amenities like a 29-person crew on board, plus a sauna, helipad, swimming pool, beauty salon, theater, gym, massage room, beach club, an outdoor bar, and even a Turkish bath, just to name a few. The 10 guest rooms include a master suite (with its own private hot tub and study) and three VIP staterooms. Wenge wood, bamboo, and marble make up the palatial interior. And the best part? It comes with Wi-Fi! So expect Jenner and her gang to be Instagramming it up while they party down.

Sailing on this massive entity, which according to TMZ is expected to hit the Mediterranean Sea, would cost guests about $1,249,500 per week.

We all already know that Jenner is a fan of extravagance and treating her friends. In her latest rendezvous, she flew her entourage via private jet to Turks and Caicos, where they all looked picture-perfect and sun-kissed.
