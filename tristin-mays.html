Biography

Tristin Mays was born in New Orleans, LA on June 10, 1990. Most famous for her childhood roles as Robin Dixon and Shaina in, respectively, the ABC series Alias and the Nickelodeon show Gullah Gullah Island. She has also guest-starred in episodes of Everybody Hates Chris, The Newsroom, Zeke and Luther and The Vampire Diaries. She and Vanessa Baden appeared together in both Gullah Gullah Island and a 2011 web series titled FAIL. During her early childhood years, she signed with the Ford Modeling Agency and appeared in several national commercials.
