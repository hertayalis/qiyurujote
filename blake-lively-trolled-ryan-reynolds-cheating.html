Blake Lively Trolls Ryan Reynolds About Cheating on Him with Her Costar

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds, king and queen of Insta-trolling, are back at it again. And this time, they've dragged Anna Kendrick into the mix.

Last night, Lively posted a promotional poster of her and Kendrick's new movie, A Simple Favor. She captioned the post, "@annakendrick47 is the hotter, female(r) version of my husband... so, would it reaaaally count as cheating??"

Kendrick responded, "So glad we're finally taking this public. I let Ryan have Deadpool, he can give me this."

Ryan's response to all of this? "The most ambitious crossover event in history. I'll miss you both. Tell my story." Seems like Ryan is down for the cheating as long as it's with Anna.

This obviously isn't the first time Lively and Reynolds have been caught in a hilarious back-and-fourth on social media. They were recently caught talking about Ryan's fake twin brother, and joking about their mutual crush on Hugh Jackman. One of Ryan's best jabs was when he tried to get answers out of his wife about the plot of her new movie and using their personal life as blackmail.

Lively and costar Kendrick have been teasing the mystery thriller together for months. Lively stars in the film as Emily Nelson and Kendrick plays her best friend Stephanie Ward, a mommy blogger. Back in May, Lively deleted all of her Instagram posts and unfollowed everyone (including Reynolds) and then filled her feed with movie teasers. She also followed 36 real people who go by her character's name.

It seems like the movie has brought the two much closer than just co-workers.

A Simple Favor hits theaters on September 14.
