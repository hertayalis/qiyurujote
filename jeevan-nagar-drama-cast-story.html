Jeevan Nagar Drama Story

The story of the drama serial Jeevan Nagar revolves around the people living in a town called Jeevan Nagar. Babbar Shah is the hope for the people of Jeevan Nagar who saved them from all goons.

The impact of issues like gender stereotypes, identity crises, and social class Division on society has been captured in a very beautiful way in the drama. Sohail Ahmed and Rabia Butt performed the character of Babbar Shah and Munni respectively in the drama. Sohail Ahmed has played the dual character in the drama serial Jeevan Nagar.
