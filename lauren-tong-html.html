Lauren Tong - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-09

TikTok star and content creator who is best known for posting lip sync and dance trend performances as well as POV videos. She often posts content that highlights her physique. Her TikTok account itslaaurr has accumulated 110,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She began posting content on Instagram in September 2021.

Trivia

In 2023, she was a guest on the Whatever podcast. She posts lifestyle and fashion content on her itslaaurr Instagram page, where she has amassed over 120,000 followers. 

Family Life

She resides in Los Angeles, California. 

Associated With

In April 2023, she collaborated on a lip sync performance with Katelyn Elizabeth. She is good friends with TikTok star Rachel Brockman.
