Kylie Jenner Goes for Gold in a Shimmery Off-the-Shoulder Gown

Publish date: 2024-06-03

For her family’s annual blowout Christmas Eve party, Kylie Jenner proved you don’t have to wait until New Year’s Eve to put on your most extravagant sequined dress.

The cosmetics mogul looked fabulous in a custom Balenciaga creation: a long-sleeved off-the-shoulder gown completely covered in shiny gold sequins. She let the dress do most of the talking by eschewing flashy jewelry. Instead, Jenner went for Old Hollywood glamour by styling her jet-black hair in a side part and down, in long, luscious curls. She further coordinated with a few black accessories, including large sunglasses and glossy stiletto pumps.

In addition to sharing photos of her incredible Christmas look on Instagram, the mom of two also posted a video montage of her and her family’s outfits to TikTok, with mom Kris Jenner and sisters Kendall Jenner, Khloé Kardashian, and Kourtney Kardashian all making cameos. “We lost Kim,” Jenner joked in the caption.

The Kardashian-Jenner Christmas Eve bash was star-studded, with a guest list that included stars like Paris Hilton, Gabrielle Union, and Megan Fox. Also in attendance was Jenner’s boyfriend, Timothée Chalamet, who made a stealthy under-the-radar appearance.

Kim Kardashian described the annual festivities during a 2019 episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians. “Our Christmas Eve party was always this fun party that I just remember being friends and family and my grandparents and cousins. It was just the best night ever,” she said. “We want it to be a place that we can just have fun, and it’s all of our friends, and definitely family and my mom’s friends, too. But a good mix of both, so we can have a good time and really just enjoy each other.”
