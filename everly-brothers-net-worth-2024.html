Introduction to the Everly Brothers

The Everly Brothers, consisting of Don and Phil Everly, were an American country-influenced rock and roll duo, known for their steel-string acoustic guitar playing and close harmony singing. They became chart-topping musicians with hits like “Bye Bye Love,” “All I Have to Do Is Dream,” and “Wake Up Little Susie.” Their influence on the music industry has been profound, impacting artists across various genres. As we look ahead to 2024, fans and industry analysts alike are curious about the net worth of the Everly Brothers’ estate and the legacy they continue to build.
