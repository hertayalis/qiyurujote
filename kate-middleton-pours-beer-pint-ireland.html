Kate Middleton Pours the Perfect Pint of Beer in Belfast, Ireland

Publish date: 2024-08-31
  • Kate Middleton and Prince William poured out pints of beer at Empire Music Hall last night.
  • The visit was part of the couple's two-day trip to Northern Ireland, celebrating its young people.
  • Kate was said to have started a girls-only drinking club in college.

People who think royals only pour themselves cups of tea have clearly never seen the Duchess of Cambridge pour herself a pint of beer right at the bar. Last night, the future queen consort also known as Kate Middleton briefly played bartender at Belfast's Empire Music Hall during a visit with husband Prince William. The evening event was part of the couple's two-day trip to Northern Ireland, in honor of the nation's young people.

Samir Hussein//Getty Images

Samir Hussein//Getty Images

Unfortunately, there's no footage of the duchess chugging down the pint on her own (bottoms up!), however, she is seen serving the perfectly-poured glass across the bar once she's done. Prince William followed suit. Despite the divey atmosphere, Kate looked every bit princess-like in a glittery green Missoni dress and nude pumps. What bar is she hitting up next? Can we stop by for a drink or two?

The duchess isn't exactly a newbie at the bar. In college, she founded a girls-only drinking club because she was "annoyed that the old ones excluded women," The Guardian reported back in 2007, but "no one remembers her getting very drunk."

She and Prince William have been seen sipping on pints of Guinness during past St. Patrick's Days, which they've spent visiting soldiers. However, the couple might not actually be the biggest ale aficionados.

RICHARD POHLE//Getty Images

In 2011, royal correspondent Victoria Murphy reported that the Cambridges weren't going to serve beer at their wedding reception. Part of the reason was that it's not quite a Queen-appropriate beverage, but another factor was that "neither Kate nor William is a big beer drinker," according to a source.

Regardless of her drink of choice, it's nice to see the duchess let loose in the royal spotlight every once in a while.
