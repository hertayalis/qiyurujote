Samuel Bower - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-11

Sound engineer and guitarist most well known as a member of the five-piece English band Counterfeit. The band rose to popularity with the release of its debut single "Come Get Some" to SoundCloud in 2015. They released their first album, Together We Are Stronger, in 2017. 

Before Fame

Months before the release of their debut single, Counterfeit released the debut YouTube video simply titled "WE ARE COUNTERFEIT" in June 2015.

Trivia

One of Counterfeit's most popular songs on YouTube is "Letter to the Lost." In 2017, they won the Best Live Act at the AIM Awards. 

Family Life

His brother Jamie Campbell Bower is the frontman of Counterfeit.

Associated With

In January 2016, he shared the stage with One Night Only's George Craig.
